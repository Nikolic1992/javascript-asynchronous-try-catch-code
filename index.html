German Shepherd

German Shepherds are one of the smartest dog breeds. They were made famous during World War I.

Pitbull

A pit bull is a fighting dog developed in 19th-century England, Scotland, and Ireland from bulldog and terrier ancestry for hunting.

Akita

Akita is a breed of working dog that originated in the mountains of northern Japan. In 1931 the Japanese government designated the breed as a “natural monument.”
